About Us

Uzhavaralayam: Revolutionizing Tamil Nadu's Agriculture and Dairy.

Uzhavaralayam , a Farmer Producer Company (FPC) from Tamil Nadu, is dedicated to transforming both agriculture and dairy sectors through education, collaboration, and unwavering quality standards, with a strong focus on women empowerment. Join us in fostering empowerment and driving positive change across the agricultural ecosystem. Together, let's build a sustainable and innovative future for farming and dairy production, ensuring equal opportunities for women. Explore collaboration opportunities with us and become part of our journey towards transformative impact in both sectors.

Join as an Uzhavan in our collective pursuit of cultivating empowerment and instigating positive change within the agricultural ecosystem. By uniting forces, we can contribute to the realization of a more sustainable, prosperous, and innovative future for the agricultural landscape. Explore collaboration opportunities with us as we navigate towards a future where agriculture becomes a catalyst for enduring positive impact.
